ü©∏ Pad-a-Girl Campaign

"A few coins could fund a pad and protect a girl from shame."

The Challenge:

Thousands of girls in Zimbabwe miss school every month because they cannot afford menstrual products. This perpetuates the cycle of poverty and denies them their right to education.

Our Solution:

  • Community fundraising tournaments
  • Direct distribution to schools
  • Education on menstrual hygiene
  • Breaking the stigma around menstruation

Impact So Far:

576 pads collected through our netball tournament with 6 participating schools
